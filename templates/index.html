How to Use

About Us

Geosphere is an online globe view tool that allows you to click anywhere on the globe and stream the relevant countries name, temperature, carbon and weather data through API calls.

Instructions

To begin, use the [ALT] key along with [MOUSE DOWN] to drag around the globe to a desirable view. Then click with [MOUSE DOWN] to zoom in on the location of choice, and stream the data. Once your done, MOUSE DOWN on the globe again to be pulled back out of the view.

For the best experience please utilise FULLSCREEN or WINDOWED MODE for all features of the application to work optimisably
