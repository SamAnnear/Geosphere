How to Use

About Us

Geosphere is an online globe view tool that allows you to click anywhere on the globe and stream the relevant countries name, temperature, location and weather data through API calls utilising longitude and latitude characteristics. Geosphere is accurate to within roughly 30km

Instructions

To begin, use the [ALT] key along with [MOUSE DOWN] to drag around the globe to a desirable view. Then click with [MOUSE DOWN] to zoom in on the location of choice, and stream the data. Once your done, [MOUSE DOWN] on the globe again to be pulled back out of the view. Alternativley, you can also utilise our "Search" key at the top to search for a particular city of your choice on the globe.

For the best experience please utilise FULLSCREEN for all features of the application to work optimisably
