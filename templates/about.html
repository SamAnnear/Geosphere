SO WHAT IS GEOSPHERE?

Geosphere is an online planet viewer that allows you to click anywhere on the globe and retrieve the country, weather data and other graphical based data from the point of click. Its main goal is to utilise a simplistic API software and allow users to retrieve weather data around the globe in just one application, instead of researching through web-page after web-page.
